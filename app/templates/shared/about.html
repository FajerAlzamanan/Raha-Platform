About Raha

AI-powered periodontal analysis platform built for dental researchers, clinicians, and biomedical scientists who need fast, reliable assessment of micro-CT scans.

Get Started Contact Us

Our Mission

Periodontal disease affects nearly half the global adult population, yet diagnosis often relies on manual inspection of complex imaging data. Raha bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI segmentation research and practical clinical tooling — making bone-loss quantification, inflammation scoring, and lesion mapping accessible to every researcher.

IAU Research Project 2025–2026. IRB-2020-02-300.
